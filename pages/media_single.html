More National Acclaim for Hylas Yachts

August 27, 2023

The winners of the Cruising World’s “Boat of the Year” awards have just been announced and the Hylas H57 has won best cruising yacht overall. This is a great testament to Bill Dixon’s design and the Hylas unwavering commitment to building high quality ocean capable yachts.

What a spectacular way to round off a hugely successful fall boat show season. The Hylas H57 won “Best In Show” at the Newport International Boat Show and now the top award after the rigorous inspection by Cruising World judges in Annapolis.
